— Base 01 · Lazio

Rome — swords & animals

Sept 6 – 24

Big, exciting, but one-thing-a-day. The arrival block, taken slow.

Via Appia · everyone's favorite

Gladiator school — Scuola Gladiatori Roma

Minimum age 6 — perfect for Keir · ~€130 · English · ~2 hrs

Tunic on, train with a wooden sword, learn real gladiator moves from a costumed instructor, then a mini-tournament and a certificate. A full morning's adventure and a highlight for a 9-year-old.

gruppostoricoromano.it
Cerveteri · ~45 min · animals

Educational farm — Bambini e Fattoria

9-yo is the target · feed ponies, alpacas, donkeys, goats · €15 kids

Feed and meet the animals, alpaca walks, pony rides — a gentle, hands-on morning built for younger kids. (Sun/holidays.)

bambiniefattoria.it
Parco di Veio · ~20 km N

Falconry experience

Family-marketed · from €50 · confirm Keir's hands-on role

Handle and free-fly birds of prey with a falconer — kids are mesmerized by the hawks on the glove. Confirm the 9-yo's hands-on part when booking.

freedome.it · falconry
— Base 02 · Emilia-Romagna

Bologna — food park & caves

Sept 24 – Oct 15

A 9-year-old's dream base — a giant food playground, plus one of Europe's great caves a couple of hours away. All flat-and-easy (which also suits Grandma's Oct 1–14 visit).

The perfect Keir day

Grand Tour Italia (the old FICO food park)

All ages · Luna Farm rides + indoor adventure park · entry free

Farm animals, tractors, pizza and gelato made in front of you, rides and a skating rink. A whole day that feels like play but is really about where food comes from — flat and stroller-easy. ⚠ Re-confirm it's open ~1 month out (finances have been shaky).

grandtouritalia.it
Genga (Marche) · ~2.5 hr · weekend

Grotte di Frasassi — standard cave tour

No minimum age — all ages · ages 6–14 €14 · ~1h15 · paved & lit

One of Europe's largest cave halls, walked on lit paved paths with a shuttle in. Bring a layer (14°C inside). The big boys can do the adventurous speleo route from the same site — see Adventures.

frasassi.com
Weekend · with the brothers

A castle with real armor

All ages · drawbridges, towers, suits of armor

The Emilia-Romagna castle circuit — Keir gets the knight fantasy, the big boys get the history.

castelliemiliaromagna.it
— Base 03 · Veneto + Dolomites

Lido di Venezia — glass, treetops & beach

Oct 15 – Nov 5

Watching, a little making, and a beach to run on when the day's one big thing is done.

Murano · jaw-drop + try it

Watch glassblowing + make a glass bead

Furnace demo all ages to watch · bead-making age 10+ (email re: a 9-yo)

A maestro turns molten glass into a galloping horse in two minutes — mesmerizing. Then VivoVenetia's torch-bead class lets you make your own ("no strength, no risk"). It's officially 10+, so email to ask about Keir at nine.

vivovenetia.com
Dolomites weekend · ~2 hr N · best all-ages

Treetop adventure park — Parco Avventura Caralte

From age 3 · ziplines, trampolines, lake boats · courses from €10

Aerial high-ropes courses, ziplines and trampolines scaled by age — the strongest all-ages pick, with easy courses for Keir and harder ones for the teens. (Skip "Tree Village Claut" — that's lodging, not a ropes park.)

cadoreavventura.com
Right outside the door

Lido beach + vaporetto rides

All ages · free run-around time

The Lido is an actual beach island — free play, and the boats themselves are a thrill. The low-key counterweight to a big-activity day.

italiakids.com · Venice for kids
— Base 04 · Campania

Naples — pizza & ponies

Nov 5 – 19

The whole family's pizza city — and a 9-year-old gets to stretch and bake his own, plus ride a horse on a volcano.

Kids welcome · all ages

Make-your-own pizza (+ gelato) in Naples

"Ages 0–99," with a parent · English · ~€50–60

Stretch the dough, add toppings, watch it bake in a wood oven in 90 seconds — then make gelato. The tastiest, most kid-paced activity of the trip, in the city that invented pizza.

viator.com · Naples pizza & gelato
Vesuvius slopes · daily

Horseback ride on Vesuvius

Age 3+ rides own horse (supervised) · €79 · Naples pickup

A guided trail ride on the volcano's flanks — gentle, supervised, and a thrill for a 9-year-old. Weather-dependent; confirm by WhatsApp.

horseridingnaples.com
Pompeii · built for kids

Pompeii with an archaeologist (kids' tour)

Age 6+ · clue booklets, gladiator barracks · EU under-18 free entry

A kid-paced, archaeologist-led tour with treasure-hunt booklets — the buried Roman city made exciting rather than exhausting.

livtours.com · Pompeii for kids
Keep the pace Keir's rule across every base: book one anchor activity per day and leave the rest open for parks, gelato, and rest. An overscheduled 9-year-old melts down; a well-paced one is the happiest traveler in the family.
