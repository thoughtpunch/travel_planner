— Base 02 · Emilia-Romagna · FOOD CAPITAL

Bologna — and a food-capital birthday

Sept 24 – Oct 15

Grey turns 12 in the literal food capital of Italy — birthplace of tagliatelle, tortellini, Parmigiano, balsamic, and the modern gelato machine. The best hands-on food on the trip.

Oct 12 · birthday move · book ahead

Grand Tour Italia — the giant food park

All ages · Luna Farm rides + indoor adventure park · park entry free

The huge food destination just outside Bologna (this is the former FICO Eataly World, reopened in 2024 as Grand Tour Italia) — pizza and gelato made in front of you, working farms and factories, rides, a skating rink. A 12th-birthday playground built entirely of food. ⚠ Finances have been shaky — re-confirm it's open ~1 month before the trip.

grandtouritalia.it
Central Bologna · the classic · sells out

Hands-on pasta — Taste Bologna

Kids 4–10 at their own station (€59) · all ages · English · ~3 hrs

Make tagliatelle, tortellini and tortelloni by hand — the signature skill of the region, and a real keeper. Book ahead; it sells out.

tastebologna.net
Anzola dell'Emilia · gelato HQ

Carpigiani Gelato Museum — family workshop

Designed for kids · book ahead · only 2 English tours/day (11:30, 14:30)

Emilia is where modern gelato is engineered. The Gelato Museum runs hands-on "gelato for families" masterclasses — churn, balance, and taste your own batch. Reserve in advance; English slots are limited.

gelatomuseum.com
Near Modena · the famous morning tour

Parmigiano Reggiano dairy tour — Caseificio 4 Madonne

Lesignana site all-ages/stroller-friendly · adults €22, kids 6–10 €12 · book ahead

Watch the king of cheeses made at dawn, then taste it by age. Mandatory advance booking; the Lesignana location is the kid-friendliest.

parmigianoreggiano.com · book a dairy
Modena · free · English page

Balsamic vinegar acetaia tour — Acetaia di Giorgio

Aging-attic tour + DOP tasting · free for individuals · book by email

See real traditional balsamic age in barrels in an 1860 home, then taste the syrupy DOP stuff (nothing like supermarket balsamic). Free for individual visitors.

acetaiadigiorgio.it
Piazza Maggiore · sweet

Chocolate masterclass — Venchi

All ages · make-your-own praline · book on Venchi site

A fun, low-stakes chocolate workshop in the center — tasting plus making your own pralines. Easy birthday-week treat.

venchi.com · workshops

— Base 04 · Campania · THE PIZZA

Naples — the holy grail

Nov 5 – 19

For a pizza-obsessed kid, this is the summit of the trip — learning real Neapolitan pizza in the city that invented it, from the people who guard the craft.

Naples · the official school · book ahead

AVPN — "Pizzaiolo Napoletano for a day"

Children 5+ welcome · €97 adult / €57 child (5–14) · 3 hrs · English

The Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana defines true Neapolitan pizza — and this is their hands-on family course (not the pro certification). Dough, the wood oven, the 60–90-second bake. The definitive pizza experience for Grey, and the strongest, fully-verified Naples option.

pizzanapoletana.org · pizzaiolo for a day
Naples · all ages · best value

Pizza + gelato making class

"Ages 0–99," under-18 with parent · ~€50–60 · English · free cancel 24h

Stretch and bake your own pizza in a wood oven, then make gelato — a genuinely all-ages combo and the best gelato-making fit in Naples. Book via Viator/TripAdvisor.

viator.com · Naples pizza & gelato
Capaccio-Paestum · ~1 hr · watch animals

Buffalo mozzarella at Tenuta Vannulo

Working farm, kids welcome · mornings only · ~€10pp

Watch buffalo mozzarella pulled by hand at an organic farm with a museum and tasting. ⚠ Phone/email booking only (Italian-leaning) — reserve days ahead, mornings, bring cash.

tenutavannulo.com
Naples · dessert

Gelato + sfogliatella tasting

Gentle walking food tour · English · all ages

Close the loop with Naples gelato and the city's famous crispy shell pastry — the sweet end of Grey's food education.

cesarine.com
