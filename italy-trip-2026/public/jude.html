— Base 02 · Emilia-Romagna · MOTOR VALLEY

Bologna — supercar heaven

Sept 24 – Oct 15

The reason Bologna is a base. Every legendary Italian marque was born within an hour, and every one runs a museum or a live factory tour. Booking shortcut: Maserati, Pagani, Stanguellini and custom car days all route through one official operator — Modenatur.

Maranello + Modena · the big one

Ferrari Museums Pass — both museums, one ticket

Ages 9 · 12 · 16 · 18 · under-19 €12, under-6 free · valid 48 hrs

One pass covers the Museo Ferrari in Maranello and the Enzo Ferrari Museum in Modena — F1 cars, the Hall of Victories, the founder's story. ~18 km apart; easiest with a car (a shuttle exists for ticket-holders).

ferrari.com · museums pass
Sant'Agata Bolognese · book early

Lamborghini MUDETEC + factory line

Factory tour age 6+ (accompanied) · Mon–Fri · no phones on the line

Walk the live V12/V10 production line plus the museum. Sells out — book through Ticketlandia from the official page.

lamborghini.com · museum
Bologna · in-city, easiest

Ducati Museum + factory tour

No hard minimum · under-18 accompanied · €32 combined

Watch motorcycles built on the line, right in Bologna — no day trip. ⚠ A renovation closure was listed Mar 30–Jul 1 2026 (ends before the trip) — confirm reopening when booking.

ducati.com · Borgo Panigale
Modena · book ahead

Maserati factory tour

Age 7+ (dedicated 7–17 tier) · €50 / €35 youth · Mon–Fri, closed Aug

~75-min plant tour + showroom where the GranTurismo is assembled. The "18+ only" claim online is wrong — kids 7+ are welcome. Book via Modenatur.

maseratifactorytour.modenatur.it
San Cesario sul Panaro

Museo & Atelier Pagani

Museum walk-in €18 · factory tour €65 (ages 13–18 €25) · 3–12 free

Horacio Pagani's Zonda and Huayra hypercars, where carbon-fibre becomes art. The atelier tour goes onto the build floor. Tickets via Modenatur.

modenatur.it · Pagani tour
Maranello · the thrill · ride only

Ride shotgun in a Ferrari / Lambo — Pushstart

Jude rides (no licence needed) · drive needs 21+ & licence

Pro-tutored road drives and Autodromo di Modena track sessions; their 4-seat Ferraris let Jude ride in back while a licensed adult drives. From ~€120. ⚠ No operator publishes a passenger age floor — WhatsApp to confirm a 16-yo rides before paying.

pushstart.it
Modena · real restoration

Stanguellini — historic workshop & museum

Family tour all ages · book info@stanguellini.it

A family-run racing marque with an active restoration shop — the closest "watch real vintage-car restoration and meet the family" experience. Confirm restoration-floor access when booking.

stanguellini.it
Finale Emilia · ~50 min

Go-karting at Extrema Kart

Jude on adult karts · (9-yo only if ≥130 cm) · book by phone

Outdoor rental circuit in the heart of Motor Valley — the closest the boys get to actually driving fast. ⚠ Italian-leaning phone booking — have a translator app / ask for English.

extremakart.it
A Motor Valley game-plan Ducati is in-city (any afternoon). Ferrari (Maranello + Modena) is one full day. Lamborghini + Pagani pair into another. Maserati's factory tour sets the calendar — book it first, then build the rest around it. All five marques are bookable; none is defunct.
— Day trips · Tuscany · THE ROCK RUN

Gemstones & the earth

do these Sept–Oct · several close ~early Nov

The rockhounding half of Jude. Tuscany's coast and islands are where Italy's minerals, marble, and gems come out of the ground. Schedule Elba and Carrara for September–October — several operators close around the start of November.

Isola d'Elba · you keep what you find

Parco Minerario dell'Isola d'Elba (Rio Marina)

All ages · kids get a hammer & keep their samples · ~€16 / €10 child

Elba is a mineralogist's legend — tourmaline, beryl, pyrite, hematite. A little mine-train tour and a supervised "raccolta minerali" collecting session where you hunt and take specimens home. The single best rockhounding day in Italy for Jude.

parcominelba.it
Capoliveri, Elba · go underground

Miniere di Calamita / Ginevro

Surface route age 5+ · underground & geologist tour age 12+

The only underground mine on Elba — descend real galleries in a helmet with a guide, or take the 3-hr geologist-led tour. Jude does it all.

minieredicalamita.it
Carrara · the marble mountains

Carrara Marble Tour (4×4 quarry)

No minimum · child tiers €25 (6–12) · season ~Mar–Nov 2

Ride an open-top Land Rover up into the blinding-white quarries Michelangelo sourced from, right into the working cuts. (The "14+" rule online is the cruise-ship version, not this operator.)

carraramarbletour.it
Pietrasanta · make something

Marble carving workshop

Beginner-friendly · email to confirm a 16-yo

Hands-on marble sculpture in tiny groups in the sculptors' town next to Carrara — turn the rock obsession into an actual carved piece. Email ahead about Jude's age and a fall date.

carving-stone.com/pietrasanta
Florence · gem in the hand

Le Arti Orafe — 1-day stone-setting

Hands-on · keep your ring · confirm 16-yo by email

Set a real gemstone onto a silver ring and take it home — the most accessible "work with gems" day in Italy (true lapidary/faceting courses here run multi-week in Italian).

artiorafe.it · craft experience
Near Verona · legal fossils

Museo dei Fossili di Bolca

Split slate & legally keep your finds · child rate 7–14 · ~€5

The one place you can hunt fossils and keep them — wild fossil collecting is illegal in Italy (they're state property), so this supervised quarry-museum is the legit way. ⚠ Posted a maintenance closure from Nov 2025 — verify it has reopened.

museodeifossili.it
Two ground rules Distance: Elba is a ferry from Piombino — best as an overnight or long day, slotted in the Rome phase or a dedicated Tuscany weekend. Fossils: tell the boys not to pocket fossils from trails, beaches, or quarries — it's illegal in Italy. Bolca is the only "keep it" exception.
— Anywhere · rainy-day rock fixes

Mineral museums

cheap · mostly weekday mornings

All cheap, all open in 2026, all near a base — great backups when the weather kills a quarry day.

Bologna · FREE

Museo Bombicci (mineralogy) + Museo Capellini (geology)

Bombicci free · meteorites, amber, UV-fluorescent minerals · Tue–Fri 9–13

48,000 specimens five minutes from a 26-m Diplodocus cast. Top pure-mineral stop, and both combine in one weekday morning.

sma.unibo.it
Florence · La Specola

Mineralogy at La Specola

Medici hardstones · "Fiori della Terra" into 2026 · €10/€5 · book online

The mineral collection is back on display after ~150 years. Sells out — book ahead.

sma.unifi.it · La Specola
Naples · Vesuvius minerals

Real Museo Mineralogico

45,000+ minerals · richest Vesuvius collection · €2.50 · weekdays

Two ~480 kg quartz crystals and the best volcanic-mineral collection anywhere — the perfect primer before the Vesuvius hike.

Real Museo Mineralogico, Naples
Torre del Greco · near Naples

Carve a cameo at Aucella

Carve your own cameo · ~$69 · historic factory

Torre del Greco has carved coral and shell cameos for centuries — make your own at a historic factory. A neat stone-craft fit near the Naples base.

aucella.com
— Base 04 · Campania

Naples — a living volcano

Nov 5 – 19

Pizza for the whole family — and for the rockhound, the most dramatic geology of the trip.

Geology · the crater rim · book official only

Mount Vesuvius crater hike (Gran Cono, Trail 5)

No minimum age · difficulty "low" · €10 · timed ticket mandatory

Walk to the rim of the volcano that buried Pompeii — black lava, sulphur vents, volcanic minerals underfoot. Closed-toe shoes essential. ⚠ Buy only at vesuviopark.vivaticket.it — resellers mark the €10 ticket up to €35.

vesuviopark.vivaticket.it
