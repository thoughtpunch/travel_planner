— Near Bologna / Florence

Fire & blades

Scarperia, in the Mugello hills north of Florence, has been a knife-making town for 700 years. It's the trip's best "hot metal" stop. (No bookable plate-armor workshop exists in Italy — the knife forge is the real hands-on fire option.)

Scarperia · the marquee

Forge demo at the Bottega del Coltellinaio

Hot work: 12 · 16 · 18 · 9-yo likely watch-only (confirm) · €15 · reserve via Pro Loco

The town's last original cutler's shop — watch a master at the forge and try it yourself. A real forge, so confirm the 9-year-old's role. ⚠ Italian-leaning — book via Pro Loco Scarperia; bring a translator.

tuscanymove.com · forge demo
Tuscany · full day · make & keep

Forge your own knife — SOS2012

16 · 18 clean · 12 likely · 9-yo confirm · ~€100 · take the knife home

A genuine forge-your-own-blade day with heat treatment. ⚠ Published 2026 dates are spring — email for an Oct/Nov session, or it won't fit. Italian-leaning.

sos2012.it
Scarperia · no fire

Knife Museum (Museo dei Ferri Taglienti)

All four boys · hands-on knife-assembly bench · walk-in · closed Tue/Wed

Interactive knife museum in a medieval palazzo with an assembly bench — the all-ages, no-flame companion to the forge.

museoferritaglientiscarperia.it
— Rome · Florence · Venice

Armor & gladiators

Swords, plate armor, and a chance to actually fight like a Roman — the boys' core obsession, all verified.

Rome · Via Appia · hands-on

Gladiator school — Scuola Gladiatori Roma

Min age 6 — all four boys · ~€130 · English · 2 hrs + certificate

History in their museum, then tunic, Roman warm-up, swordplay, and a mini-tournament. The single best "be a warrior" experience, and ideal for the 9-year-old.

gruppostoricoromano.it
Florence · day trip

Stibbert Museum — armor & armies

All four boys · €10/€7 · timed escorted entry · closed Thu

A hall of life-size mounted armored knights, ~16,000 arms and a superb samurai collection. Pair it with Scarperia for one "blades + armor" Florence day.

museostibbert.it
Venice · no extra ticket

Doge's Palace Armoury

All four boys · on the standard Doge's Palace route

2,000+ arms and armor — Henry IV's armor, Lepanto battle trophies — included on the Doge's Palace visit. Book timed entry via official MUVE/Vivaticket.

palazzoducale.visitmuve.it
— Murano · Venice/Lido base

Glass & fire (Murano)

Murano splits neatly by age: the furnace for the teens, the torch-bead bench for the younger ones.

Murano · the furnace

Glassblowing class — Wave Murano Glass

16 · 18 yes · 12 borderline (confirm) · 9 too young · ~€255–287 · English

A real beginner furnace class with a maestro (~2 hrs). Age sources conflict 12+ vs 16+ — confirm in writing for the 12-year-old.

wavemuranoglass.com
Murano · the torch · younger kids

Glass-bead lampworking — VivoVenetia

12 · 16 · 18 · age 10+ (9-yo: email for an exception) · ~€54 · "no strength, no risk"

Make a glass bead at a torch with a parent present — the best younger-kid glass option. Pick the bead product (their furnace class is 15+).

vivovenetia.com
— Rome · Tuscany · Naples

Animals & falconry

Birds of prey on the glove, trail rides on a volcano, and farms full of animals to feed.

Parco di Veio · near Rome

Falconry experience

Family-marketed, likely all four · from €50 · English · confirm 9-yo's hands-on role

~3 hours handling and free-flying hawks and falcons with a falconer. (A bigger 4-hr version near Pisa has 50+ birds and an explicit 10+ rule with a younger-kid accompanied tier.)

freedome.it · falconry
Naples · Vesuvius slopes

Horseback riding on Vesuvius

Age 3+ rides own horse (supervised) — all four · €79 · Naples pickup

Guided trail rides on the volcano's flanks, daily. Weather-dependent — confirm by WhatsApp.

horseridingnaples.com
Cerveteri · near Rome

Educational farm — Bambini e Fattoria

9-yo is the target · ponies, alpacas, donkeys, goats · kids €15

Feed the animals, alpaca walks, pony rides — gentle and hands-on. (For more, the social-farm network is fattoriesociali.it.)

bambiniefattoria.it
— Marche · Tuscany · Puglia

Caves underground

Frasassi is the standout — and it perfectly splits the group: the 9-year-old does the easy show-cave tour while the older three go off-path caving from the same site.

Genga (Marche) · ~2.5 hr from Bologna

Grotte di Frasassi — standard tour

No minimum — all four boys · ages 6–14 €14 · ~1h15 · paved & lit

One of Europe's largest cave halls, walked on lit paths with a shuttle in. 14°C inside — bring a layer.

frasassi.com
Same site · off-path caving

Frasassi Speleo-Adventure (Blue / Red)

12 → Blue · 16 · 18 → both (Red has a 30 m rope shaft) · 9 excluded (hard 12+)

Guided off-path caving, gear provided — a hard age-12 floor for insurance. The perfect split-the-group day: 9-yo does the standard tour while the older three crawl.

frasassi.com · speleo-adventure
Garfagnana (Tuscany) · ~2 hr

Grotta del Vento

Route 1 (~1 hr) all ages · Route 3 (~3 hr, 1,195 steps) for the fit

Three nested routes by difficulty. In low season (parts of Nov) often only the easy Route 1 runs — fine for the whole family.

grottadelvento.com
— Dolomites · Motor Valley

Heights & speed

Cabled mountain routes, treetop courses, and karts — pick by age. The Dolomites are ~2 hours north of Venice/Lido.

Cortina · beginner route

Via ferrata "Ra Pegna" — Guide Alpine Cortina

Min age 7 — all four boys · from €110 · gear & IFMGA guide included

A 2-hour guided beginner cabled route with an official mountain guide. Book this specific route — many Dolomite ferratas are 12+.

guidecortina.com
Cadore · best all-ages

Treetop park — Parco Avventura Caralte

From age 3 · ziplines, trampolines, lake boats · courses from €10

Aerial high-ropes courses scaled by age — easy ones for Keir, hard ones for the teens. (Not "Tree Village Claut" — that's lodging.)

cadoreavventura.com
Finale Emilia · near Bologna

Go-karting — Extrema Kart

Kids' kart ages 7–12 (min 130 cm) · 16 & 18 on adult karts

Outdoor rental circuit in Motor Valley — the closest the boys get to driving fast. ⚠ Italian-leaning phone booking — measure the 9-yo (needs 130 cm) and confirm a Sept–Nov open day.

extremakart.it
